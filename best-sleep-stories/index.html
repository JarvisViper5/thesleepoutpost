Frequently Asked Questions

What are sleep stories for adults?

Sleep stories are long, gentle narratives (usually 40-55 minutes) designed specifically to help adults fall asleep. Unlike exciting audiobooks, they use slow pacing, calming imagery, and repetitive, soothing language patterns that naturally quiet the mind and signal your body it's time to rest.

Are these sleep stories really free?

Yes — every story is available free on our YouTube channel. No subscription needed. We also offer downloadable story script PDFs and curated bundles for offline reading.

Which category works best for falling asleep?

It depends on your personality. Ocean and Nature stories tend to work fastest for most people thanks to rhythmic, repetitive natural sounds. Ancient and Sci-Fi stories appeal to imagination-heavy sleepers who need an engaging world to "get lost in." Fantasy stories work great for creative minds. Try one from each category and see what works for you.

How long are the stories?

Most stories run 40-55 minutes — long enough to fall asleep even if it takes a while. The pacing intentionally slows toward the end, matching your natural sleep cycle.

Can I listen on my phone?

Absolutely. All stories are on YouTube, which works on any phone. Pro tip: set a sleep timer on your phone so the audio stops after you fall asleep, saving battery and preventing accidental wake-ups from autoplay.

What's the best way to listen?

We recommend comfortable sleep headphones or a pillow speaker — regular earbuds can be uncomfortable when lying on your side. Keep the volume low, just loud enough to follow the words without straining. A dark room with your phone face-down works best.
