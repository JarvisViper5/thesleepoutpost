Space Station Chronicles Ep. 1 the Arrival

A sleep story for adults · Designed to help you fall asleep · ~45 minutes

Read the Opening

Tonight begins something new. You find yourself arriving at a deep-space research station with hydroponic gardens, observation lounges, quiet laboratories, sleeping quarters with viewport ceilings, and long curving corridors that follow the station's gentle rotation. The familiar details greet you like old friends, and the night opens gently ahead.

Ahead, the path widens and the light softens. You let your breathing settle, listening to the smallest sounds while blue nebula glow reflecting on glass blends into dim corridor lights and calm air. Your steps become quieter, your jaw unclenches, and your shoulders drop. With every exhale, feel your body grow a little heavier, a little quieter, a little more at rest.

You walk slowly through this place you are beginning to know well, space station chronicles — ep. 1: the arrival. The air carries slow rotation of ring shadows, and every turn opens into a steady hum from distant engines. Nearby, the old astronomer who tends the telescope array reminds you why you keep coming back. Let your shoulders soften and let your breath come and go without effort.
